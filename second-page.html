Why is mental health important?

Importance of mental health

According to the CDC, mental and physical health are equally important in terms of overall health. Mental illness, specifically depression, can increase the risks for other physical health problems such as strokes and heart disease. It's important to note that mental health can change over time, so it's never too late to take steps to improve it.

KEY WORDS

  • Anxiety:
    Intense, excessive, and persistent worry and fear about everyday situations.
  • Depression:
    A constant feeling of sadness and loss of interest, which stops you doing your normal activities.
  • Eating disorder:
    Illnesses in which the people experience severe disturbances in their eating behaviors and related thoughts and emotions.
  • Mental illness:
    A wide range of conditions that affect mood, thinking, and behavior.
