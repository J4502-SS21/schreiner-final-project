Managing anxiety

Anxiety is a common feeling each person has to deal with throughout their life; whether it's anxiety about school, work, or person relationships, there are a few practices you can try to incorporate into your life on the daily for overall better mental health.

  1. Exercise
  2. Meditate
  3. Take time for yourself
